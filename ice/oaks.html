Dallin H. Oaks

1st Counselor in the First Presidency

  • Born: Aug 12, 1932
  • Apostle: Apr 7, 1984
  • Place: Provo, Utah

"Our priorities are most visible in how we use our time."

President Oaks was sustained as a member of the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles on April 7, 1984. He has served as a counselor in the First Presidency since 2018.
