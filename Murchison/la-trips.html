About Los Angeles Trips

Los Angeles is surrounded by many great destinations that are perfect for weekend trips. Visitors can explore beaches, mountains, and desert landscapes without traveling very far.

Santa Barbara

Santa Barbara is about two hours from Los Angeles and is known for its beautiful beaches, Spanish-style buildings, and great restaurants.

Big Bear Lake

Big Bear Lake is a mountain destination where visitors can hike, ski, and enjoy outdoor activities year round.

Joshua Tree National Park

Joshua Tree National Park is famous for its desert scenery, hiking trails, and unique rock formations.

Laguna Beach

Laguna Beach is a relaxing coastal town known for ocean views, art galleries, and beautiful beaches.
